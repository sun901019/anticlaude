Problem

I wanted one controllable operator system, not disconnected tools.

  • AI output had no governance or approval discipline.
  • Ecommerce pricing and sourcing depended on ad hoc spreadsheets.
  • Content operations, review flows, notifications, and dashboards were fragmented.
  • Operator actions were difficult to trace, audit, and improve.
The project goal was not to make another chat UI. It was to turn real work into workflows, approvals, artifacts, and domain logic.
